Machine Learning on the Features of Happiness


Machine Learning Features


Random Forest Algorithm: What are your friends worth?

The happiness dataset from 2020 and 2021 has been used in a Machine Learning context to predict the happiness score based on the features.

Random Forest (a type of Machine Learning algorithm) was used to determine the importance of each feature, better depicted in the word cloud below. At a glance, social support and GDP are the most influential features of happiness.
