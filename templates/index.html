A guide to the modern life surrounded by technology

Balance is Key

With copious amounts of technology and data available in the modern era, many people in today's society are overwhelmed. White collars often find themselves doing long hours filtering excelsheets or compiling the next best powerpoint package that will make or break a company's future. I remember using Powerpoint in 1st grade (26 years ago), so yes, Powerpoint is rather ancient and incredibly successful considering that the average life span of a company these days is less than 18 years.

As appropriate, do challenge the status quo, use new tools and technologies as they are available to help us live better and relieve us of as much hardship as possible.
- Turn a 10 hour job to a 1 hour job with pandas.
- Make predictions of the future with machine learning.

My point really is to highlight that as a society, we should never cease to be creative and always encourage a growth mindset. With that said, nothing is possible without good health so do keep wellbeing close by like a breath of fresh air because it won't prevail with attention when around but when you don't have it, without a doubt, you will go down.

Ultimately, I hope you find your passion, your flow and live a balanced and fruitful life.
